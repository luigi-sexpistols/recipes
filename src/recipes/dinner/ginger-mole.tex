\section{Ginger Mole}
  Serve as the sauce for a beef dish such as tacos or steak.
  \begin{multicols}{2}
    \subsection{Ingredients}
      \begin{itemize}
        \item 5 dried Ancho or Guanjillo chillies
        \item 1 dried Pasilla chilli
        \item \sfrac{1}{2} an onion
        \item 2 cloves garlic
        \item 4-6 tbsp grated ginger
        \item \sfrac{1}{4} cup sesame seeds
        \item 1 tsp peppercorns
        \item 2 cups water
        \item 1-2 tbsp cocoa powder
        \item 1-2 tbsp brown sugar
      \end{itemize}
  \vfill\null
  \columnbreak
  \subsection{Method}
    \begin{enumerate}
      \item Toast sesame seeds, making sure not to burn them.
      \item Grill onion, tomato, and garlic until starting to blacken (around 5 minutes).
      \item Peel skin off tomato and garlic.
      \item Add all ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
      \item Pour into a pan and simmer until reduced.
    \end{enumerate}
  \end{multicols}
