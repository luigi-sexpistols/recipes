\section{Beef Tacos}

\index{meat!beef!steak}
\index{country!Mexico}
\index{starch!tortilla}
\index{starch!bread}

  These tacos take advantage of the ginger mole and pico de gallo recipes found in this book. You can also make the tortillas from this book, or buy small (15cm) tortillas.
  \begin{multicols}{2}
    \subsection{Ingredients}
      \begin{itemize}
        \item 450g beef fillet or other steak cut
        \item 150g Ricotta cheese
        \item 2 avocados (optional)
      \end{itemize}
  \vfill\null
  \columnbreak
  \subsection{Method}
    \begin{enumerate}
      \item Prepare tortilla dough, if making your own.
      \item Prepare the pico de gallo, set aside.
      \item Cut the beef into thin strips, set aside.
      \item If you have avocado, slice it and add a small amount of citrus juice to stop it browning.
      \item Prepare the ginger mole.
      \item While the mole is reducing, fry the beef quickly over very high heat and cook the tortillas.
      \item Serve tortillas, pico de gallo, mole, beef, Ricotta, and avocado separately for construction at the table.
    \end{enumerate}
  \end{multicols}
